Project Title(CoronaGo)

As we all know Novel-Corona virus is spreading among the peoples exponentially. Thousands of souls died due to this Epidemic. Whole world is now Suffering due to this, Economy of World is going Down day by day. There is a need to break the chain of infection before it goes to 3rd stage i.e. stage of community transfer. For the same, many countries including India has implemented lock-down. This lock-down can only be made successful if people of these countries follow it properly and maintain social distancing. To do this we need an information system which can provide following information:
  1. Real time information of COVID-19 infected persons in a particular area of search. This information is very useful for Health-workers and other essential services workers like doctors, nurses, policeman, milkman etc. as they will be able to find out the areas where they have to take extra precautions while working.
  2. People should be able to easily identify that they should go for COVID-19 pathological test or not on the basis of symptoms and with the help of that information system. This system can help not only health-workers but also general public to identify probable victim COVID-19 in the early stage which not only helps in spreading it but also in quick recovery of the infected person

The main objective of our project is to provide real time information to general public regarding infected persons in their area. Details of objectives are given below:
  • Person can primarily identify that whether he/she is having symptoms of COVID-19 or not. So that, he/she should go for COVID-19 pathological test or not.
  • People can get the real time information regarding the infected persons in the area of radius of maximum 5 Km from the desired search area.
  • You can also have the worldwide information of persons infected from COVID-19.

  • Get CheckUp
  • Get Notification
  • Find
  • About Project
  • Contact Us

On Get CheckUp Page some Attribute are regarding symptoms of patient will be asked. On the basis of the Machine Learning model suggest, one should go for testing or not. On GetNotification Page one can get real time Covid 19 Patients data and also, one can search by Country Name. On Find Page one can get how many people are infected in 5 Km Radius?

There is some Limitations of this project.
  • Internet is basic requirement to use this project.
  • This project is designed to run on modern web browsers. So, may cannot give desired results with old browsers.
  • For more accurate results, we need to have more data of corona -ve persons so that training of ML module can be done in more efficient way.
